# VS-010 Evidence: 01 - Scope and Boundaries

## Purpose

Record one controlled real-provider live smoke through the existing API, Repair
Mentor, and AI Gateway path.

## In scope

- founder-reported safe live-smoke metadata;
- deterministic baseline and repository validation results;
- documentation and evidence only;
- repository validation for VS-010 artifacts and secret safety.

## Boundaries

- No provider, AI Gateway, Repair Mentor, endpoint, schema, migration, or
  dependency behavior changed.
- No prompt-quality or repair-quality evaluation was performed.
- No credential or raw provider response is recorded.
- Human verification remains required.
- Final diagnosis and repair approval remain unavailable.
- Knowledge Service and shared/global knowledge are not implemented.
